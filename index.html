Why now
We've already built the hard parts. The launcher, naming tool, and AI flows exist — they just live in the wrong place.
Zapier has a ceiling. It can't run logic, share data between automations, or scale into a campaign engine.
The AI opportunity is real. A fully autonomous campaign agent that strategises, creates and runs ads doesn't exist off the shelf at our scale.
We own everything we build. No vendor lock-in, no per-seat pricing — the platform compounds with every tool we add.
What this replaces
Zapier automations — replaced by scheduled jobs we own, with proper logic and full logging.
Google Apps Scripts — replaced by a real web app with shared state, team auth, and a database.
Manual Ads Manager checks — replaced by the change log, pacing monitor, and optimisation engine.
Scattered test notes & tribal knowledge — replaced by the Hypothesis Lab, a permanent searchable backlog of what we've learned.
